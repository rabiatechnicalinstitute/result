Our Programs

Explore our comprehensive diploma programs designed to build expertise and career opportunities in technical fields.

Diploma in Fire and Safety Engineering

Learn advanced fire safety systems, emergency management, and protective equipment design. This program provides comprehensive knowledge in fire prevention, control systems and occupational safety.

Key Topics:

  • Fire Prevention & Control Systems
  • Occupational Safety Standards
  • Emergency Management
  • Safety Equipment Design
Duration: 3 Years

Diploma in Mechanical Engineering

Master mechanical systems, machine design, and manufacturing processes. Develop skills in thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and mechanical maintenance across various industries.

Key Topics:

  • Machine Design & Analysis
  • Thermodynamics & Fluid Mechanics
  • Manufacturing Processes
  • Industrial Maintenance
Duration: 3 Years

Diploma in Electrical and Electronics Engineering

Explore electrical circuits, power systems, and electronic devices. Gain expertise in installation, maintenance, and troubleshooting of electrical and electronic equipment.

Key Topics:

  • Electrical Circuits & Power Systems
  • Electronic Devices & Components
  • Installation & Maintenance
  • Troubleshooting Techniques
Duration: 3 Years
